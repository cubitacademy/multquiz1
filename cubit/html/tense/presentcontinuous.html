Present Continuous

Subject  +   is/am/are   +   verb+ing

I am
He , She , It is
We , You , They are
Uses
  • To denote an action that started in the past and still doing
    • Iam teaching
    We are listening
  • To denote a planned future act
    • My father is coming from the UK tomorrow
